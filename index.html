Welcome

This project explores the fascinating journey of HTTP, the foundation of the modern internet. From its academic roots to modern high-performance protocols like HTTP/3, discover how this technology shapes our digital life. Understanding this evolution is crucial because every web request, streaming service, and app you use today still fundamentally depends on these underlying mechanics. Across this site, you will find a breakdown of HTTP's history, an analysis of its modern iterations, and a glossary of key terms to help clarify these technical concepts.

Key Terms

HTTP, TCP, UDP, Latency, Multiplexing
